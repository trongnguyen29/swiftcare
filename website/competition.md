# Official Website Directory: AI Medical Scribe & Clinical AI Vendors

## TL;DR
- All 23 requested products/companies have verified official websites; the cleanest way to organize them is by go-to-market model: enterprise ambient scribes, SMB/self-serve scribes, EHR-native AI, infrastructure/platform, behavioral-health-specific, and decision-support-adjacent.
- Three notable consolidations: Augmedix and Athelas are now both Commure companies (Commure's all-cash acquisition of Augmedix valued it at ~$139 million and closed Oct. 2, 2024; the Commure–Athelas merger closed end of October 2023 at a reported $6 billion combined valuation); "Scribble" is not a standalone company but IKS Health's product suite; and Nuance's DAX Copilot has been unified into Microsoft Dragon Copilot.
- Several entries are EHR-native features rather than standalone vendors (Epic's "Art" AI Charting, Oracle Health Clinical AI Agent, athenahealth Ambient Notes, Zoom Clinical Notes), and athenahealth's Ambient Notes is a marketplace that embeds third-party scribes including Abridge and Suki.

## Key Findings

### Enterprise Ambient Scribes (health-system focused)
- **Abridge** — https://www.abridge.com/ (product page: https://www.abridge.com/product) — Generative-AI ambient documentation for health systems with deep Epic integration and "Linked Evidence" traceability.
- **Microsoft Dragon Copilot (Nuance DAX Copilot / Dragon Medical One)** — https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/health-solutions/clinical-workflow/dragon-copilot — Microsoft's unified AI clinical assistant. Per Microsoft (Mar. 3, 2025), Dragon Copilot is "the first AI assistant for clinical workflow that brings together the trusted natural language voice dictation capabilities of DMO with the ambient listening capabilities of DAX"; it became generally available in the U.S. and Canada in May 2025. DAX Copilot support content remains on support.microsoft.com.
- **Suki (Suki AI)** — https://www.suki.ai/ — Voice-enabled ambient AI assistant doing documentation, coding, and Q&A, plus the Suki Platform for partners.
- **Ambience Healthcare** — https://www.ambiencehealthcare.com/ — Enterprise ambient AI platform (AutoScribe, AutoCDI) tuned for 100+ specialties. Per Fierce Healthcare, "after a six-month pilot period that pitted five major ambient scribes against each other, the Cleveland Clinic selected … Ambience," announcing an exclusive five-year partnership at ViVE 2025.
- **DeepScribe** — https://www.deepscribe.ai/ — Ambient AI scribe specializing in specialty care, especially oncology.
- **Augmedix (and Augmedix Go)** — https://www.augmedix.com/ — Ambient AI documentation suite (Augmedix Go, Assist, Live); now a Commure company.
- **Nabla** — https://www.nabla.com/ — AI clinical assistant ("Copilot") combining ambient scribe with broader clinical-assistant capabilities. Per Fierce Healthcare, Nabla is rolling out to 10,000 doctors across The Permanente Medical Group (TPMG) in Northern California, documented in the NEJM Catalyst paper "Ambient Artificial Intelligence Scribes to Alleviate the Burden of Clinical Documentation."

### SMB / Self-Serve Scribes (clinician- and small-practice-focused)
- **Heidi Health** — https://www.heidihealth.com/ (US: https://www.heidihealth.com/en-us) — Self-serve AI scribe/"AI Care Partner" for clinicians across many countries and specialties.
- **Freed (Freed AI)** — https://www.getfreed.ai/ — Self-serve ambient AI scribe focused on community clinics and individual clinicians.
- **OrbDoc** — https://orbdoc.com/ — AI medical scribe app for physicians (mobile-first; consumer app "OrbVoice").
- **Notev (NoteV)** — https://www.notev.ai/ — Self-serve voice-to-note ambient documentation platform for clinicians, priced from a low monthly fee.

### EHR-Native AI (features inside the EHR)
- **Epic ("Art" / AI Charting)** — https://www.epic.com/software/ai-clinicians/ — "Art" is Epic's clinician-facing AI brand; its ambient documentation capability is "AI Charting," which automatically drafts documentation, surfaces relevant information, and queues orders based on patient conversations.
- **Oracle Health (Oracle Clinical AI Agent)** — https://www.oracle.com/health/clinical-suite/clinical-ai-agent/ — Voice-enabled AI agent integrated into Oracle Health's EHR for note generation, order creation, and chart review (formerly the Clinical Digital Assistant).
- **athenahealth (Ambient Notes)** — https://www.athenahealth.com/solutions/ambient-notes — Ambient listening + generative-AI note generation embedded in athenaOne Mobile; a "choose-your-player" marketplace that embeds partners including Abridge and Suki.
- **Zoom (Zoom Clinical Notes)** — https://www.zoom.com/en/industry/healthcare/solutions/clinical-notes/ — Ambient AI scribe within Zoom Workplace for Clinicians for in-person and telehealth visit documentation.

### Infrastructure / Platform
- **Commure** — https://www.commure.com/ — AI-native enterprise RCM and ambient documentation platform; parent of Augmedix and Athelas.
- **AWS HealthScribe (Amazon)** — https://aws.amazon.com/healthscribe/ — HIPAA-eligible API service for software vendors to build clinical apps that transcribe and summarize patient-clinician conversations.
- **Athelas** — https://www.athelas.com/ — AI platform for RCM, RPM, and ambient documentation; merged with Commure (now "Athelas, powered by Commure").
- **IKS Health (Scribble / Scribble Now / Swift / Pro)** — https://ikshealth.com/ (Scribble suite: https://ikshealth.com/clinical-support-solutions/clinical-documentation/ambient-ai-scribing/) — Care-enablement company whose Scribble Suite blends generative AI with human-in-the-loop review.
- **Scribble** — Not a separate company. "Scribble" is IKS Health's branded documentation product suite (Scribble Now, Scribble Swift, Scribble Pro, Scribble Transcribe, Scribble Live).

### Behavioral-Health-Specific
- **Mentalyc** — https://www.mentalyc.com/ — AI documentation built specifically for therapists/behavioral health (progress notes, treatment plans, progress tracking).

### Decision-Support-Adjacent / Multi-Function
- **Glass Health** — https://glass.health/ — Evidence-grounded clinical AI for ambient scribing and clinical decision support (differentials, plans), plus a developer API.
- **Vero (Vero Scribe)** — https://www.veroscribe.com/ — AI medical scribe, charting, and documentation platform (clinical documentation product; entity "Vero Scribe Inc.").

## Details

**Consolidation and renaming notes (important for accuracy):**
- **Augmedix → Commure:** Augmedix announced the definitive merger agreement on July 19, 2024, and the deal closed October 2, 2024. Per Augmedix's release, it was "an all-cash transaction that values Augmedix at approximately $139 million in equity value," with stockholders receiving "$2.35 per share in cash" — a premium of ~169% over the 30-day VWAP ending July 18, 2024. Augmedix's site now identifies it as "Augmedix, a Commure company." Augmedix Go (clinician-controlled mobile ambient app) remains live alongside Augmedix Assist and Augmedix Live.
- **Athelas → Commure:** The Commure–Athelas merger (Oct. 2023) was backed by a $70 million investment from General Catalyst at a reported $6 billion valuation for the combined entity, which retained the Commure name and is led by Athelas co-founder/CEO Tanay Tandon. Both athelas.com and commure.com remain active.
- **DAX Copilot → Dragon Copilot:** Microsoft unified Nuance's DAX Copilot and Dragon Medical One into "Microsoft Dragon Copilot." The most specific official product page is the Microsoft for Healthcare Dragon Copilot page; legacy DAX Copilot support/training content still lives on support.microsoft.com.
- **Scribble = IKS Health product:** There is no standalone "Scribble" company; it is IKS Health's clinical documentation suite, sold as Scribble Now (real-time GenAI note), Scribble Swift (1-hour, human-in-the-loop), Scribble Pro (4-hour, clinician-reconciled with E/M coding), plus Scribble Transcribe and Scribble Live.

**EHR-native vs. standalone:** Epic, Oracle Health, athenahealth, and Zoom entries are features within larger platforms rather than independent scribe vendors. athenahealth's Ambient Notes is particularly notable because it is a marketplace that embeds third-party AI scribes (Abridge, Suki, and others), so an "athenahealth scribe" may actually be powered by another vendor on this list.

**Aggregators to avoid:** Many high-ranking search results are third-party review/aggregator sites (e.g., deepcura.com reviews, elion.health, intuitionlabs.ai, zoominfo.com, capterra.com, tooldirectory.ai, welcome.ai). These are not official and were excluded from the URLs above.

## Recommendations
- **For procurement/vendor outreach:** Use the official homepages listed above. For the four EHR-native entries, go to the specific product pages (Epic AI Clinicians, Oracle Health Clinical AI Agent, athenahealth Ambient Notes, Zoom Clinical Notes) rather than the corporate root domains.
- **For the consolidated entities (Augmedix, Athelas):** Confirm current contracting through Commure, since commercial/sales motions may route through the parent.
- **For "Scribble":** Direct all inquiries to IKS Health; do not search for a separate Scribble company.
- **Benchmark/threshold to revisit:** If you are building a living directory, re-verify the Microsoft (Dragon Copilot rebrand), Epic ("Art" rollout), and athenahealth Ambient Notes marketplace entries quarterly, as these are evolving fastest. Trigger a re-check if any of these vendors announces a new acquisition, a brand change, or an EHR-marketplace partner addition.

## Caveats
- "Art" is Epic's official AI-for-clinicians brand; the user's reference to an "Art" feature is correct as a brand, but the expansion "ART = Automated Review of Transcripts" could not be verified and appears to be a misnomer — Epic's ambient documentation capability is officially called "AI Charting."
- Valuations, customer counts, and funding figures cited by search results vary by source and date; the figures included here (Augmedix $139M, Commure–Athelas $6B/$70M, Permanente 10,000 doctors) come from the companies' own releases or Fierce Healthcare/NEJM Catalyst reporting, but should be treated as point-in-time.
- Vero Scribe is a smaller/newer vendor; its official site (veroscribe.com) was verified, but it has a thinner public footprint than the larger enterprise players.
- The Epic, athenahealth Ambient Notes, and Zoom Clinical Notes product-page URLs were verified by a research pass but not independently re-fetched in this session; if exact-link precision is mission-critical, confirm by navigating from the vendor's root domain (epic.com, athenahealth.com, zoom.com).
